# Frequently Asked Questions

## Booking a ride
**How do I book a ride?**
Request a ride by providing your pickup and drop-off locations and
choosing a payment method (CASH or WALLET). The fare is calculated and
shown to you before the ride is confirmed.

**How is a driver assigned to me?**
After you request a ride, a nearby available driver is matched to your
request. The driver can then accept the ride, after which it is
confirmed.

## During and after the ride
**What are the possible ride statuses?**
A ride moves through statuses such as CONFIRMED (a driver has accepted),
and then ENDED once the trip is complete. A ride that is called off has
the status CANCELLED.

**How do I pay?**
If you chose CASH, you pay the driver at the end of the trip. If you
chose WALLET, the fare is deducted from your wallet balance when the ride
ends.

## Ratings
**How do ratings work?**
After a ride ends, the rider can rate the driver and the driver can rate
the rider. Ratings are given on a scale of 1 to 5 stars. A driver's
overall rating is the average of the ratings they have received.

**Can I change a rating?**
A rating for a completed ride can only be submitted once and cannot be
changed afterwards.

## Account and support
**Who can I contact for help?**
For issues the assistant cannot resolve — disputes, incorrect charges,
or account problems — contact support through the app.

**What is the difference between a rider and a driver account?**
A rider requests and pays for rides. A driver accepts ride requests,
completes trips, and receives earnings into their wallet. Drivers are
onboarded with a vehicle before they can accept rides.
