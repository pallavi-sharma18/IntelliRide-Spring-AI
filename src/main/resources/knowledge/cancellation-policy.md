# Cancellation Policy

> NOTE: The numbers below (free-cancellation window, fee amount) are not
> enforced anywhere in the codebase yet. They are sensible defaults —
> edit them to match the policy you actually want before relying on them.

## Cancelling a ride
You can cancel a ride after requesting it. A ride can be cancelled while
it is still being arranged or before it starts. Once a ride is cancelled
its status becomes CANCELLED and it cannot be resumed; you would need to
request a new ride.

## Free cancellation window
You can cancel free of charge within **2 minutes** of requesting a ride,
as long as the driver has not already started travelling to your pickup.

## Cancellation fee
If you cancel after the free window, or after a driver is already on the
way to your pickup, a cancellation fee of **30** may apply. The fee
covers the driver's time and travel.

## Who can cancel
Both riders and drivers can cancel a ride. If a driver cancels, you are
not charged a cancellation fee and you can request another ride.

## Refunds for cancellation
If you were charged a cancellation fee through your wallet and believe it
was applied in error, you can request a review through support.
