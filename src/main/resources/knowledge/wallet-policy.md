# Wallet Policy

## What the wallet is
Every user has a wallet linked to their account. The wallet is used to
pay for rides and to receive earnings (for drivers).

## Payment methods
There are two ways to pay for a ride:

- **CASH** – pay the driver directly at the end of the ride.
- **WALLET** – the fare is deducted automatically from your wallet
  balance when the ride ends.

You choose the payment method when you request the ride.

## Adding money to the wallet
You can top up your wallet at any time. The amount you add is credited
to your wallet balance and recorded as a wallet transaction.

## Paying with the wallet
When a ride ends and the payment method is WALLET, the fare is deducted
from your wallet balance. Your wallet must have enough balance to cover
the fare; otherwise the WALLET payment cannot be completed and you may
need to top up or pay by cash.

## Withdrawing money
You can withdraw your full wallet balance at any time. Withdrawals move
the entire available balance out of the wallet.

## Transaction history
Every credit (top-up, earnings) and debit (ride payment, withdrawal) is
recorded as a wallet transaction so you can review your wallet activity.
